Pantene's collaboration with Rent the Runway. Worked with RTR to create 8 different looks for 4 stylish Cities: LA, NY, Austin and Miami. Each city had a day and a night look. Rent the runway styled clothing and Pantene styled hair. When girls ordered from Rent the runway, they got a sample of our new dry shampoo spray. For each of the looks we created how –To videos that lived on both our web site and our youtube page as well as a static step by step tutorial on pinterest. We used Instagram to show our favorite outfits and their product pairings. We got the word out on twitter by using our hashtag, #wantThatHair.
