Concept and Launch of Pantene’s Youtube series "Put Pantene to the Test." Twist it, bend it, braid it, heat it up or blow it out – we put a lot of stress on our hair every single day. In this series we put Pantene to the test in variety of ways that mimic the everyday demands our tresses constantly face. Our superior science shows how Pantene gives your hair the tools it needs to withstand anything you can throw at it.
